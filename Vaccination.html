Flu Vaccine Registration

The seasonal flu vaccine is now available. Click below for more details.

Pneumococcal Vaccine

Pneumococcal vaccination is recommended for individuals over 65 and those with chronic conditions. Check with your healthcare provider.

Hepatitis B Vaccination

Protect yourself from Hepatitis B with the vaccination. Available at hospitals and clinics nearby.

HPV (Human Papillomavirus) Vaccine

The HPV vaccine helps protect against cervical and other cancers. Available for both men and women.

Tetanus, Diphtheria, and Pertussis (Tdap) Vaccine

Ensure your Tdap vaccination is up to date. Recommended for adults every 10 years.

Vaccines Not Currently Available

Malaria Vaccine

The Malaria vaccine is under research and not widely available at the moment. Stay tuned for updates.

HIV Vaccine

Currently, there is no approved HIV vaccine. Research is ongoing, but it is not yet available for public use.

Herpes Vaccine

There is no approved vaccine for herpes yet. Clinical trials are underway, but availability is uncertain.
